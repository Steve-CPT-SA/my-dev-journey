About the Course
I took part in the AWS AI & ML Challenge, where I explored the core concepts of artificial intelligence and machine learning, including generative AI, responsible AI, and practical ML techniques.

Project: Adaptive Productivity Coach
As part of the challenge, I used prompt engineering on AWS PartyRock to build an AI-powered productivity app.
What It Does
The app takes in four user inputs and generates a structured, personalised plan:

1.Task — what the user wants to accomplish
2.Time — how much time is available
3.Energy — current energy level (1–10)
4.Focus Type — Deep Work, Learning, Light Tasks, Creative, Administrative, or Physical

The output includes an adaptive step-by-step plan, a focus mode, a minimum win, and what to avoid — all tailored to the user's current state.

Why I Built It
This app came from my own experience — working full time as an electrician while studying and coding after hours to transition into tech. Managing time and deciding what to do next without burning out is itself exhausting. I built this to solve that problem.
